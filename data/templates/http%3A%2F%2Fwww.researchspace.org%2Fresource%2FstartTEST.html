    What it is about
    artresearch.net provides open and consolidated access to images and metadata from partners of PHAROS, the International Consortium of Photo Archives.
    The collections document works of art and architecture and the history of photography itself, together with nearly a century of associated scholarly documentation.
    See our about page for more information and a guide to using the platform.
