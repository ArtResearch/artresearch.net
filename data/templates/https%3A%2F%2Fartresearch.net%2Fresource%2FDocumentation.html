Simple Search

Simple Search is a simple search engine to find Artworks, Artists, and many other things in our platform. We use the solar engine to provide you the best results to your input search.

Advanced Search

Advanced Search is a more complicated search engine for you to add more info to your search. You can add the info in specific topics you you want to include in your search.

Contextual Search

Contextual Search is a Structured Search functionality where you can choose the subject you want to search and eliminate the results with extra arguments.
