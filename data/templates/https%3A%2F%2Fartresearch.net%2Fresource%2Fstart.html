Discover artworks through the lens of photo archives.

The collections of Pharos, the International Association of Photo Archives, document works of art and architecture and the history of photography itself. They contain more than of a century of scholarly documentation. artresearch.net provides the tools to access, research, and contextualize these resources for the 21st century.

We are committed to creating an open and freely accessible digital research platform allowing for comprehensive consolidated access to photo archive images and their associated scholarly documentation.
The PHAROS collections collectively contain an estimated 25 million images documenting works of art and architecture and the history of photography itself. Please visit our statistics page to view the number of records that are currently available online.
