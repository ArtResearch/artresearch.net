Report Error in the Platform

From any artwork you can report an error in from the yellow button "Report Error" on the top-right of the info.
You can add an Error providing the Type of Error you want.
There are 4 Types of Error for the Artworks for the moment, "Data Error", "License Error", "Image Error", "Matching Error" and "Other".
In each of them you need to provide a Title and a Description, and press Save to submit your Error.
Then other Collection Curators will view the error and find a solution to the error.
