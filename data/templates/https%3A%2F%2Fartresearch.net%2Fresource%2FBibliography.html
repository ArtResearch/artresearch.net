Bibliography
on Photo Archives

The following bibliography gathers key scholarly works on photo archives and their multifaceted roles in the production, preservation, and interpretation of art historical knowledge. It includes foundational texts on the history and theory of photo archives, studies on their materiality and institutional contexts, as well as recent contributions addressing their digitization, reconciliation in Linked Open Data environments, and use in digital art history. Additional sections focus on the intersections of photography with art history, decolonial perspectives on archival practices, and approaches drawn from visual anthropology and material culture studies.
